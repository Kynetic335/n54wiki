# Boost

The Boost section covers the N54 turbo system end to end: the stock twin turbos and their limits, wastegates and boost actuators, the charge path (chargepipe, intercooler), common boost faults, and the upgrade ladder (hybrids, single turbo). It also covers boost control concepts like WGDC and the MAP/TMAP sensor scaling used on bigger setups.

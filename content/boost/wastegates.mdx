# Wastegates

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Wastegates bleed exhaust around the turbine to control boost. The N54 uses internal wastegates actuated by a vacuum/pressure system and commanded via WGDC.

## Why it matters

Worn wastegates cause rattle, boost control errors, and overboost/underboost faults.

## How it works

The DME commands a wastegate duty cycle; the actuator moves the wastegate to hold the boost target.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Wastegate rattle
- Boost oscillation
- Over/underboost

## What to check in logs

- Boost target vs actual
- WGDC

## Mechanical checks first

Check wastegate arm play and actuator function before tuning.

## Related codes or fault patterns

- Underboost/overboost faults (e.g. 30FF, 2-series boost codes) — verify by scan.

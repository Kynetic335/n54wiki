# MSD80 DME

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The MSD80 is one of the two main Siemens/Continental DME (engine computer) variants used on the N54. It runs a ROM image that contains the engine calibration.

## Why it matters

Knowing whether your car has an MSD80 or MSD81 determines which ROM, definitions, and tuning approach apply.

## How it works

The DME reads sensors, runs the calibration maps in its ROM, and drives the injectors, ignition, and boost actuators to meet the torque request.

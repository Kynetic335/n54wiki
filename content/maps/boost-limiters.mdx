# Boost Limiters

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

> Educational reference only. This page does not publish tune values.

## What this map controls

Boost limiter maps cap allowable boost for protection. They can clamp boost below the requested target under certain conditions.

## Why it matters

Limiters protect the engine; raising them without supporting hardware removes safety margin and can cause damage.

## Related log channels

- Boost target vs actual
- WGDC

## What changes are visible in logs

A limiter shows as boost being clamped below request under specific conditions.

## What can go wrong if changed blindly

- Removing protection margin
- Overboost if mis-set
- Limp mode

## Mechanical problems that can look like a tuning problem

- Boost leaks
- Worn turbos masking the true ceiling

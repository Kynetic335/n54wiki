# Exhaust Cam Timing

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Exhaust cam timing is the VANOS-controlled position of the exhaust camshaft, influencing spool and scavenging.

## Why it matters

Exhaust timing affects turbo spool especially; deviations indicate VANOS issues.

## How it works

The DME targets an exhaust cam angle; VANOS actuates it via oil pressure and a solenoid.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Target vs actual deviation
- Spool/performance changes

## What to check in logs

- Exhaust VANOS target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Confirm VANOS health before adjusting exhaust cam tables.

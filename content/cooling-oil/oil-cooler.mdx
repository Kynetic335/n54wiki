# Oil Cooler

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The oil cooler removes heat from the engine oil. Adequate oil cooling matters for sustained high-load use.

## Why it matters

High oil temps degrade protection and indicate the engine is working hard; upgraded cooling helps on track or big builds.

## How it works

Oil passes through a cooler (often coolant- or air-based) to shed heat.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- High oil temps under sustained load

## What to check in logs

- Oil temp

## Mechanical checks first

Verify oil cooler function and consider upgrade for hard use.

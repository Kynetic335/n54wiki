# Single Turbo

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A single-turbo conversion replaces the twin turbos with one larger turbocharger and a new manifold/charge setup for high-power builds.

## Why it matters

Single-turbo setups offer the highest airflow ceiling but trade away some low-end spool and require extensive supporting work.

## How it works

One large turbo with an external wastegate (typically) feeds the engine; boost control and fueling are built around it.

# Vacuum Lines

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The N54 boost-control system relies on vacuum lines and components that crack and leak with age, disrupting boost control.

## Why it matters

Vacuum leaks cause erratic boost and faults that are easily mistaken for tuning problems.

## How it works

Cracked or disconnected lines change how the wastegate actuators respond.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Erratic boost
- Underboost
- Boost oscillation

## What to check in logs

- Boost target vs actual
- WGDC

## Mechanical checks first

Inspect and smoke-test vacuum lines before tuning boost.

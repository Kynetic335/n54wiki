# VANOS Solenoids

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

VANOS solenoids control oil flow to the VANOS units, moving the cams. They clog or fail over time, causing VANOS faults.

## Why it matters

Dirty or failed solenoids are a common cause of VANOS codes and rough running — often fixed by cleaning or replacement, not tuning.

## How it works

The DME pulses the solenoid to route oil and move the cam to the target angle.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- VANOS fault codes
- Rough idle
- Cam position errors

## What to check in logs

- VANOS target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Clean or replace solenoids and check oil condition before tuning.

## Related codes or fault patterns

- VANOS-related codes — verify by scan.

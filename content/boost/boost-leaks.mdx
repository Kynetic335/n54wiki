# Boost Leaks

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A boost leak is any unintended escape of compressed air between the turbos and the engine — cracked pipes, loose clamps, failed DVs, or bad seals.

## Why it matters

Boost leaks cause underboost, poor response, and fault codes that are easy to mistake for tuning problems.

## How it works

Pressurized air escapes before reaching the cylinders, so actual boost falls below target.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Underboost
- Hissing under load
- Slow spool

## What to check in logs

- Boost target vs actual
- WGDC (may peg trying to compensate)

## Mechanical checks first

Smoke-test the charge path before any boost tuning.

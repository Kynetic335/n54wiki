# Misfire Diagnosis

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A misfire is a failed or incomplete combustion event in a cylinder. The DME counts misfires per cylinder and can set fault codes.

## Why it matters

Misfires under boost are common on the N54 and usually trace to ignition (coils/plugs) or fueling, not necessarily the tune.

## How it works

The DME detects crank-speed irregularities per cylinder and increments misfire counters.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Stumble
- Rough idle
- Loss of power
- Check-engine light

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters by cylinder
- Timing corrections
- Fuel trims

## Mechanical checks first

Swap-test coils/plugs and check fueling before tuning. Isolate the cylinder first.

## Related codes or fault patterns

- Misfire codes by cylinder — verify by scan.

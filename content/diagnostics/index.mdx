# Diagnostics

The Diagnostics section is the symptom-to-cause reference for the N54. Each page describes a symptom or fault code, the hardware most likely involved, and the exact log channels to check. Diagnostic pages always point you to mechanical checks before tuning changes. Fault-code numbers should be confirmed with a scan on your specific car.

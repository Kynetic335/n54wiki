# 2AAF Fuel Pump Plausibility

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

2AAF is a commonly referenced N54 fuel-pump plausibility fault. Confirm the exact definition by scanning your car.

## Why it matters

It often relates to low-side supply (LPFP) not meeting expectations relative to demand.

## How it works

The DME compares expected vs actual fuel-supply behavior and flags implausibility.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Fuel pressure issues under load
- Possible limp mode

## What to check in logs

- LPFP pressure
- Fuel rail pressure target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Test LPFP/low-side supply; verify wiring/connector.

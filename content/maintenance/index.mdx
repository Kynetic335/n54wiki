# Maintenance

The Maintenance section covers the service the N54 needs to stay reliable — especially before tuning. It includes service intervals, the key wear items (plugs, coils, fuel pumps, water pump), cleaning tasks like walnut blasting, and a pre-tune checklist. Skipping maintenance is one of the most common causes of "tuning" problems that are really worn parts.

# E30 (Stock Turbo)

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

An E30 stock-turbo path runs roughly 30% ethanol blended fuel on the factory turbos for extra knock resistance. Exact blend and fueling needs must be verified.

## Why it matters

Ethanol adds knock margin but demands more fuel volume, pushing the LPFP/HPFP harder. Blend accuracy matters.

## How it works

A build path is a coherent combination of fuel, hardware, and a matching calibration. This wiki describes the concept and prerequisites; it does not publish tune values.

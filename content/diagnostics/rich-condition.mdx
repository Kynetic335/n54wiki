# Rich Condition

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A rich condition is too much fuel for the air (lambda below target).

## Why it matters

Rich running wastes fuel, fouls plugs, and can indicate sensor or fueling calibration issues.

## How it works

Excess fuel relative to air (leaking injectors, sensor error, or calibration) makes mixture rich.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Low lambda/AFR
- Fuel smell
- Fouled plugs

## What to check in logs

- Lambda/AFR
- STFT/LTFT
- Fuel trims

## Mechanical checks first

Check for leaking injectors and sensor health.

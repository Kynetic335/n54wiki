# Injector Failure

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Injector failure (clogging, leaking, or electrical) causes misfires, rough running, and fueling errors.

## Why it matters

Failed injectors mimic tuning and ignition problems; correct coding is also required after replacement.

## How it works

A faulty injector delivers wrong fuel quantity or pattern, upsetting combustion in that cylinder.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Misfire
- Rough idle
- Fuel trim drift
- Long crank

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters by cylinder
- Fuel trims

## Mechanical checks first

Test/inspect injectors; verify coding after replacement.

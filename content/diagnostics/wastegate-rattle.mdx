# Wastegate Rattle (Diagnosis)

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Wastegate rattle is a rattling noise from worn wastegate linkages on aging stock turbos, often at light throttle.

## Why it matters

It indicates turbo wear and can correlate with boost-control inconsistency and a falling boost ceiling.

## How it works

Play in the wastegate arm lets components rattle off-boost.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Rattle at low throttle/idle off-boost
- Inconsistent boost

## What to check in logs

- Boost target vs actual
- WGDC

## Mechanical checks first

Confirm the noise source; severe rattle means turbo service, not a tune change.

# N54 Workflow Guide Rules

Workflow guides must be public, educational, and validation-oriented.

## Required Structure

- Goal.
- Audience and prerequisites.
- Required tools or observations.
- Safety and legality notes.
- Step-by-step public procedure.
- Validation checklist.
- Stop conditions.
- Source confidence label.

## Prohibited Workflow Content

- Flashing instructions.
- BIN writing, tune generation, binary patching, or checksum correction.
- Private file import/export or customer tune handling.
- Emissions defeat, monitor suppression, or fault concealment.
- Steps that depend on raw private BIN, XDF, DAMOS, A2L, OLS, or customer files.

## Tone

Use direct instructional language. Explain why each step matters when skipping it could lead to a wrong diagnosis or unsafe conclusion.

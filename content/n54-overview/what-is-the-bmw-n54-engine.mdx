# What Is the BMW N54 Engine?

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The BMW N54 is a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six gasoline engine using direct injection. It was BMW's first mass-production turbo gasoline engine of the modern era and became one of the most popular tuning platforms on the used market.

## Why it matters

Understanding what the N54 is — a direct-injection twin-turbo straight-six — frames every other topic in this wiki: fueling limits, boost control, and the DME software that ties it together.

## How it works

Two turbochargers feed an inline-six. Fuel is injected directly into each cylinder at high pressure. The DME (engine computer) coordinates fuel, boost, ignition, and torque to meet driver demand.

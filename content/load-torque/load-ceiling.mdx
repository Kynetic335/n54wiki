# Load Ceiling

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The load ceiling is the maximum load the calibration permits. It can be a protective cap or a tuning-relevant limit.

## Why it matters

If requested load is pinned at a ceiling, more boost will not help until the ceiling and supporting limiters are addressed in calibration.

## How it works

Maps and limiters bound the maximum requested/achievable load.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Requested load pinned at a fixed maximum

## What to check in logs

- Requested load
- Actual load

## Mechanical checks first

A ceiling is software; confirm it is not actually a hardware shortfall first.

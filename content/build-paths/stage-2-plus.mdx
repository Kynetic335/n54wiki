> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A Stage 2+ path pushes the stock turbos near their limit, often with added cooling/methanol or ethanol blending and full supporting bolt-ons.

## Why it matters

Near the stock-turbo ceiling, heat and fueling become the limiters. Cooling, fueling capacity, and maintenance must be solid.

## How it works

A build path is a coherent combination of fuel, hardware, and a matching calibration. This wiki describes the concept and prerequisites; it does not publish tune values.

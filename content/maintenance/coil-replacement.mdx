# Coil Replacement

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Ignition coils wear and fail over time, especially under boost. Replacement is a routine N54 maintenance item.

## Why it matters

Failing coils cause misfires that look like tuning or fueling problems.

## How it works

Coils degrade and fire weakly; swapping suspect coils isolates the issue.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Misfire under load
- Rough idle

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters by cylinder

## Mechanical checks first

Swap-test and replace failing coils; do not chase coil misfires in the tune.

# Oil Leaks

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The N54 commonly develops oil leaks at the valve cover gasket (VCG), oil filter housing gasket (OFHG), and oil pan gasket.

## Why it matters

Leaks create mess, fire risk near hot components, and can mislead diagnosis; they are normal wear items.

## How it works

Gaskets harden and fail with age/heat, letting oil seep at these joints.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Oil on the block/exhaust
- Burning oil smell
- Drips

## Mechanical checks first

Locate the source visually; replace the failed gasket.

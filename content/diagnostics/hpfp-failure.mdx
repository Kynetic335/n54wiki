# HPFP Failure

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

HPFP failure is a common N54 issue causing long crank, fuel pressure crashes, rough running, and limp mode.

## Why it matters

The HPFP is a known weak point; failure limits or stops fueling under load.

## How it works

A worn/failed HPFP cannot maintain rail pressure, especially under demand.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Long crank
- Rail pressure crash
- Limp mode
- Rough running

## What to check in logs

- Fuel rail pressure target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Confirm rail pressure behavior; replace the pump if failing. Mechanical issue.

## Related codes or fault patterns

- Fuel pressure faults (e.g. 29DC) — verify by scan.

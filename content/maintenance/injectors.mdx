# Injector Maintenance

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Injectors can foul, leak, or fail over time. Replacement requires entering the correct injector indexes (coding).

## Why it matters

Bad injectors cause rough running and misfires; correct coding is required after replacement.

## How it works

Injectors are inspected/tested and replaced as needed, then coded to the DME.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Rough idle
- Misfire
- Long crank

## What to check in logs

- Fuel trims
- Misfire counters

## Mechanical checks first

Verify coding after any injector work.

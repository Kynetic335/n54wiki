# Oil Filter Housing Gasket (OFHG)

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The oil filter housing gasket seals the oil filter housing (and can leak oil and/or coolant where it interfaces). A common N54 leak source.

## Why it matters

OFHG leaks can drop oil onto the belt/accessories and mix areas of oil and coolant; a known maintenance item.

## How it works

The gasket degrades and leaks at the housing interface.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Oil and/or coolant leak at the housing
- Oil on belt/accessories

## What to check in logs

- N/A — visual

## Mechanical checks first

Replace the OFHG; inspect belt for oil contamination.

# Injectors

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The N54 uses direct injectors that fire fuel into each cylinder. Early and later N54s used different injector designs; verify which your car has.

## Why it matters

Injector condition affects idle quality, fueling accuracy, and the ability to support more fuel on bigger builds.

## How it works

The DME pulses each injector for a calculated duration at the target rail pressure. Injector flow data is coded into the DME.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Rough idle
- Misfires
- Long crank
- Fueling drift

## What to check in logs

- Fuel trims
- Misfire counters
- Rail pressure

## Mechanical checks first

Inspect/test injectors and confirm correct coding before tuning fueling.

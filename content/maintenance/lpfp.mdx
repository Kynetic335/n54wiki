# LPFP Maintenance

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The in-tank LPFP supplies the HPFP. On higher-power or high-ethanol builds it may need upgrading; monitor low-side supply.

## Why it matters

A weak LPFP starves the HPFP and causes fuel pressure issues under load.

## How it works

Check low-side supply pressure; upgrade or replace as the build demands.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Fuel pressure drop under sustained load

## What to check in logs

- LPFP pressure
- Fuel rail pressure target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Test low-side supply; upgrade for high-demand builds.

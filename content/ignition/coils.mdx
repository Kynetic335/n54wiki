# Ignition Coils

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The N54 uses coil-on-plug ignition: one coil per cylinder fires its spark plug. Coils are a common wear item.

## Why it matters

Weak or failing coils cause misfires that look like fueling or tuning faults, especially under boost.

## How it works

The DME triggers each coil to fire its plug at the commanded timing.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Misfires (often under load)
- Rough idle
- Stumble

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters by cylinder
- Timing corrections

## Mechanical checks first

Swap-test suspect coils and inspect connectors before tuning.

## Related codes or fault patterns

- Misfire codes by cylinder — verify by scan.

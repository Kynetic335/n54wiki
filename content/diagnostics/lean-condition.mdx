# Lean Condition

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A lean condition is too little fuel for the air (lambda above target). Under boost this is dangerous.

## Why it matters

Lean under load raises knock and damage risk; common causes include fuel supply limits, leaks, or wrong ethanol content.

## How it works

If fuel cannot keep up with air (pump/injector limits, or more ethanol than the tune assumes), mixture goes lean.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- High lambda/AFR under load
- Timing pull
- Possible damage

## What to check in logs

- Lambda/AFR
- Fuel rail pressure target vs actual
- STFT/LTFT

## Mechanical checks first

Check fuel supply (HPFP/LPFP), leaks, and actual ethanol blend.

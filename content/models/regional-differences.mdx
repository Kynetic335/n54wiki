## What it is

N54 cars differ between regions (for example US vs Euro vs other markets) in available models, emissions equipment, fuel grade assumptions, and sometimes DME/ROM calibration.

## Why it matters

A tune, fuel assumption, or part that suits one market may not suit another. Region affects fuel octane baselines and emissions hardware.

## How it works

BMW calibrated and equipped cars to meet local regulations and fuel quality. The base map assumptions can differ.

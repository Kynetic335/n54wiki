# Radiator

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The radiator sheds coolant heat to the air. A clogged or damaged radiator reduces cooling capacity.

## Why it matters

Reduced radiator capacity raises coolant temps under sustained load, limiting safe power.

## How it works

Coolant passes through the core; airflow removes heat.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Rising coolant temps under load
- Overheating

## What to check in logs

- Coolant temp

## Mechanical checks first

Inspect for blockage/damage and adequate airflow.

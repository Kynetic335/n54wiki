# VANOS Overview

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

VANOS varies camshaft timing on the intake and exhaust cams. The N54 uses it to optimize spool, torque, and efficiency across rpm.

## Why it matters

VANOS position affects spool and power. Faults cause rough running, codes, and performance loss.

## How it works

Oil-pressure-actuated units, commanded by solenoids, advance/retard the cams toward DME-targeted positions.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Rough idle
- Performance loss
- VANOS fault codes

## What to check in logs

- VANOS target vs actual position if exposed

## Mechanical checks first

Check oil condition and solenoids before tuning cam tables.

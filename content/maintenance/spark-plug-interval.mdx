# Spark Plug Interval

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Tuned N54s typically need shorter spark plug intervals than stock because higher cylinder pressure stresses the spark. Exact interval depends on power level — verify by source.

## Why it matters

Worn plugs cause misfires under boost, a very common "tune" complaint that is really a maintenance issue.

## How it works

Plugs wear and gap opens over time; boost accelerates this.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Misfire under boost as plugs age

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters
- Timing corrections

## Mechanical checks first

Replace plugs at a shorter interval when tuned; verify spec/interval by source.

# Rough Idle

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Rough idle is unstable or shaky idle, with many possible causes on the N54.

## Why it matters

It often signals ignition wear, carbon buildup, VANOS issues, injector problems, or vacuum leaks.

## How it works

Any disruption to fuel, spark, air metering, or cam timing can destabilize idle.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Shaking at idle
- Misfire at idle
- Stalling

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters
- Fuel trims
- VANOS target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Check plugs/coils, vacuum lines, VANOS, and carbon buildup first.

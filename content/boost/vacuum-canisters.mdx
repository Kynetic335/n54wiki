# Vacuum Canisters

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The N54 boost-control system uses vacuum (and canisters/reservoirs) to actuate the wastegates. Cracked lines or failed canisters disrupt boost control.

## Why it matters

A vacuum leak in the boost-control system causes erratic boost, a classic "looks like a tune problem but is mechanical" issue.

## How it works

Stored vacuum lets the solenoids move the wastegate actuators predictably.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Erratic boost
- Underboost
- Boost oscillation

## What to check in logs

- Boost target vs actual
- WGDC

## Mechanical checks first

Smoke-test and inspect all vacuum lines and canisters before tuning.

# Logging Lambda / AFR

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Lambda/AFR channels show the air-fuel mixture. Lambda is fuel-agnostic; AFR is fuel-specific.

## Why it matters

Mixture under load reveals lean (dangerous) or rich conditions and confirms whether fueling matches the target.

## How it works

Oxygen/wideband sensing reports mixture; the DME targets a lambda under load.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Lean under load (high lambda)
- Rich (low lambda)

## What to check in logs

- Lambda/AFR
- STFT/LTFT
- Fuel rail pressure

## Mechanical checks first

Confirm sensor health and actual ethanol blend before judging fueling.

# Water Pump Failure

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Electric water pump failure is a common N54 issue that causes overheating, often with little warning.

## Why it matters

It can strand the car and cause heat damage; proactive replacement is widely recommended before adding power.

## How it works

The electric motor or pump fails, coolant stops circulating, and temps spike.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Overheating
- Coolant temp warning
- Limp mode

## What to check in logs

- Coolant temp

## Mechanical checks first

Replace proactively if old; this is a mechanical failure, not a tuning issue.

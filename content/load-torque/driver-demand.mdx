# Driver Demand

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Driver demand maps the accelerator pedal position to a requested torque. It shapes how the pedal feels and how aggressively torque is requested.

## Why it matters

Driver demand affects throttle response and how quickly the car asks for load/boost.

## How it works

A pedal map converts pedal angle (and rpm) into a torque request that feeds the rest of the model.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Lazy or overly aggressive pedal feel

## What to check in logs

- Pedal position
- Torque request
- Throttle angle

## Mechanical checks first

Pedal feel changes are calibration, not hardware.

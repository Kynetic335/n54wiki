# N54 Wiki

The definitive technical reference for the **BMW N54 twin-turbo inline-six** — covering everything from stock engine basics through full single-turbo builds.

Whether you just bought your first 135i or you're deep into a built-motor E82, this wiki gives you the technical foundation to tune smarter, diagnose faster, and build safer.

## About This Wiki

This wiki is maintained by and for the N54 community. All power figures, hardware recommendations, and tuning guidance are presented as **general ranges based on common community experience** — actual results depend on your vehicle's condition, fuel quality, modification list, and ambient conditions.

**Always work with a qualified tuner** when modifying engine management. This wiki educates; it does not replace professional calibration.

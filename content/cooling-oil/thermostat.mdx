# Thermostat

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The thermostat regulates coolant flow to control operating temperature. A stuck thermostat causes over- or under-cooling.

## Why it matters

Incorrect coolant temp affects performance, fueling, and protection thresholds.

## How it works

It opens/closes based on temperature (and DME control on map-controlled units) to hold target coolant temp.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Slow warm-up (stuck open)
- Overheating (stuck closed)
- Temp codes

## What to check in logs

- Coolant temp

## Mechanical checks first

Replace with the water pump if age/condition warrants.

# MSD81 DME

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The MSD81 is the other main N54 DME variant. It is generally associated with later cars; map layouts and ROM IDs differ from the MSD80.

## Why it matters

Definitions and tuning maps are DME- and ROM-specific. Applying MSD80 assumptions to an MSD81 (or vice versa) is a common mistake.

## How it works

Like the MSD80, it runs a calibration ROM and controls fuel, ignition, boost, and torque.

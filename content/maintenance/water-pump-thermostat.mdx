# Water Pump & Thermostat

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The electric water pump and thermostat are commonly replaced together as a proactive cooling-system service on the N54.

## Why it matters

Water pump failure is common and causes overheating; replacing proactively prevents being stranded and protects the engine before adding power.

## How it works

Both parts are replaced as a set when age/condition warrants.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Overheating if neglected

## What to check in logs

- Coolant temp

## Mechanical checks first

Replace proactively if old, especially before tuning.

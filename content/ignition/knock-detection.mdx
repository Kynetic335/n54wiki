# Knock Detection

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Knock detection uses knock sensors to listen for abnormal combustion (detonation). When detected, the DME retards timing to protect the engine.

## Why it matters

Knock is destructive. The detection system is the engine's defense, and its corrections are visible in logs.

## How it works

Sensors pick up the vibration signature of knock; the DME responds per cylinder by pulling timing.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Timing pull
- Power loss
- Audible knock in severe cases

## What to check in logs

- Timing corrections
- Knock signals if exposed

## Mechanical checks first

Address fuel quality, heat, plugs, and mechanical condition; do not mask knock with tuning.

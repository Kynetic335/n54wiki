# Full E85

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A full-E85 path runs high-ethanol fuel, requiring substantial fuel-system capacity (upgraded LPFP/HPFP and often port injection on big builds).

## Why it matters

E85 offers high knock resistance but demands the most fuel volume; the fuel system must be built to support it.

## How it works

A build path is a coherent combination of fuel, hardware, and a matching calibration. This wiki describes the concept and prerequisites; it does not publish tune values.

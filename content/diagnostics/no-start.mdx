# No Start

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A no-start is when the engine cranks but will not fire (or will not crank). Causes range from fuel pressure to electrical/sensor faults.

## Why it matters

Systematic diagnosis (fuel, spark, signals) is needed; fuel pressure is a common N54 culprit.

## How it works

The engine needs fuel pressure, spark, and correct sensor signals to start; any missing element prevents starting.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Cranks but no start
- No crank in some cases

## What to check in logs

- Fuel rail pressure
- Fault codes

## Mechanical checks first

Scan for codes; check fuel pressure, battery, and key sensors.

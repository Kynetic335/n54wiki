# Logging IAT

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

IAT (intake air temperature) shows the temperature of the charge air entering the engine. Rising IAT raises knock risk and timing pull.

## Why it matters

IAT explains heat-soak power loss and timing corrections; it provides essential context for any pull.

## How it works

A sensor reports charge-air temp; higher temps make the DME more conservative.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- IAT climbing pull-to-pull
- Timing pull correlated with high IAT

## What to check in logs

- IAT
- Timing corrections

## Mechanical checks first

Improve intercooling/heat management rather than masking with the tune.

## What it is

The N54 is an inline-six with an aluminum block, direct injection, and two turbochargers. Detailed dimensions (bore, stroke, compression) should be verified against a trusted source before quoting.

## Why it matters

Architecture sets the limits: how much boost and load the bottom end, head, and fueling can support before something becomes the weak link.

## How it works

Air enters through the intake, is compressed by the turbos, cooled by the intercooler, and metered into the cylinders. Fuel is injected directly; the DME controls timing and torque.

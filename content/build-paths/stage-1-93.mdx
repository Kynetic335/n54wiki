# Stage 1 (93 Octane)

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A Stage 1 path on 93-octane fuel, typically with minimal supporting hardware. 93 has more knock margin than 91.

## Why it matters

Higher octane allows a slightly more aggressive calibration than 91, but the car must still be mechanically healthy first.

## How it works

A build path is a coherent combination of fuel, hardware, and a matching calibration. This wiki describes the concept and prerequisites; it does not publish tune values.

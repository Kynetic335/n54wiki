# VANOS Faults

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

VANOS faults occur when the cam cannot reach or hold its target position, often due to solenoids, oil condition, or mechanical wear.

## Why it matters

VANOS faults cause rough running, codes, and performance loss, and can be mistaken for tuning problems.

## How it works

The DME detects cam target-vs-actual deviation and sets faults; root causes are usually mechanical/oil-related.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Rough idle
- Performance loss
- Stored VANOS codes

## What to check in logs

- VANOS target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Check oil, solenoids, and mechanical condition before any tuning change.

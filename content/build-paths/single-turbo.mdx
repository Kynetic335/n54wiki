## What it is

A single-turbo build replaces the twins with one large turbo and supporting manifold/charge hardware for the highest airflow ceiling.

## Why it matters

Highest power potential, but the most supporting work: fueling, cooling, sensors, and often a built engine for big targets.

## How it works

A build path is a coherent combination of fuel, hardware, and a matching calibration. This wiki describes the concept and prerequisites; it does not publish tune values.

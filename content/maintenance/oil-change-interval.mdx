# Oil Change Interval

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Tuned and hard-used N54s benefit from shorter oil change intervals than the factory schedule. Use the correct specification oil — verify by source.

## Why it matters

Clean oil protects the VANOS, HPFP drive, and bearings; it also keeps VANOS solenoids working.

## How it works

Oil degrades with heat and load; shorter intervals maintain protection.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- VANOS issues and wear linked to old/dirty oil

## What to check in logs

- Oil temp (context)

## Mechanical checks first

Use correct-spec oil and shorten the interval for tuned use. Verify spec by source.

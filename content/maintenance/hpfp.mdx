# HPFP Maintenance

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The HPFP is a known wear item. Maintenance means monitoring rail pressure health and replacing the pump if it fails or limits fueling.

## Why it matters

A weak HPFP causes long crank, fuel pressure crashes, and limp mode — and limits power.

## How it works

Monitor rail-pressure-hold under load; replace the pump when it cannot maintain target.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Long crank
- Rail pressure crash
- Limp mode

## What to check in logs

- Fuel rail pressure target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Confirm rail pressure holds before tuning; replace failing pumps.

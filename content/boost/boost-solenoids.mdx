# Boost Solenoids

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Boost control solenoids translate the DME's WGDC command into actuator movement by routing vacuum/pressure to the wastegate actuators.

## Why it matters

Failed or sticking solenoids cause boost control faults that mimic tuning problems.

## How it works

The DME pulses the solenoid; it routes vacuum to move the wastegate to hold the boost target.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Boost control errors
- Underboost/overboost
- Oscillation

## What to check in logs

- WGDC
- Boost target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Test solenoid function and check connectors/lines before tuning.

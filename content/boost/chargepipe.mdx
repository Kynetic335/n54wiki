# Chargepipe

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The chargepipe carries compressed air from the intercooler to the throttle body. The plastic factory chargepipe is a known crack/failure point under boost.

## Why it matters

A cracked chargepipe causes a large boost leak, underboost, and limp mode.

## How it works

It is part of the charge path; any break drops boost pressure before the throttle.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Sudden boost loss
- Loud air leak under boost
- Limp mode

## What to check in logs

- Boost target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Inspect chargepipe and connection before tuning; a metal upgrade is common.

# Oil Temps

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Oil temperature reflects how hard the engine is working and the health of oil cooling. Sustained high oil temps reduce protection.

## Why it matters

Oil temp context helps interpret pulls and decide when cooling upgrades are needed.

## How it works

A sensor reports oil temp; it rises with sustained load and inadequate cooling.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- High oil temps under sustained or track use

## What to check in logs

- Oil temp

## Mechanical checks first

Ensure correct oil and cooling capacity before hard use.

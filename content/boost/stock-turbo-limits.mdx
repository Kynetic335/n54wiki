# Stock Turbo Limits

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The stock turbos have a practical airflow ceiling. Beyond it, they make heat instead of power, and reliability suffers. Exact limits depend on fuel, hardware, and condition.

## Why it matters

Knowing the limit prevents chasing power the hardware cannot safely make and guides the upgrade decision.

## How it works

As demand exceeds the compressor map, efficiency drops: more heat, more knock risk, less usable gain.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Rising IAT and timing pull at the top
- Diminishing returns from more boost

## What to check in logs

- IAT
- Timing corrections
- Boost target vs actual

# Valve Cover Gasket (VCG)

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The valve cover gasket seals the valve cover to the head. It is a common N54 oil leak source as it ages.

## Why it matters

A leaking VCG can drip oil onto hot exhaust components (smell, smoke, fire risk) and is a frequent maintenance item.

## How it works

The gasket hardens and loses its seal, letting oil escape at the valve cover.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Oil on the head/exhaust side
- Burning smell
- Smoke from the bay

## What to check in logs

- N/A — visual

## Mechanical checks first

Replace the VCG; inspect the cover for cracks.

# Pre-Tune Checklist

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A pre-tune checklist is the set of mechanical and maintenance checks to complete before flashing any tune, so the baseline is healthy.

## Why it matters

Tuning a worn car causes problems and bad data. A healthy baseline is required for safe, accurate tuning and diagnosis.

## How it works

Confirm plugs, coils, fuel system, cooling, vacuum lines, and oil are in good shape first.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Post-tune problems that trace to skipped checks

## What to check in logs

- Baseline log to confirm health

## Mechanical checks first

Complete all mechanical checks before tuning; see the existing readiness checklist.

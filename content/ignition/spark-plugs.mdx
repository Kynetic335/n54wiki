# Spark Plugs

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Spark plugs ignite the mixture. The N54 is sensitive to plug condition and gap, especially as boost rises.

## Why it matters

Worn or wrongly gapped plugs cause misfires and break up spark under boost — frequently mistaken for tuning problems.

## How it works

The coil fires the plug across its gap; a correct, smaller gap helps maintain spark under high cylinder pressure.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Misfire under boost
- Stumble
- Rough running

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters
- Timing corrections

## Mechanical checks first

Inspect/replace plugs and verify gap before tuning. Verify gap spec by source.

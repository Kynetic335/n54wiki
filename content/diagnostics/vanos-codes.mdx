# VANOS Codes

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

VANOS codes indicate the cam cannot reach/hold its target position, usually due to solenoids, oil, or mechanical wear.

## Why it matters

These cause rough running and performance loss and are usually fixed mechanically, not in the tune.

## How it works

The DME flags target-vs-actual cam deviation and sets a code.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Rough idle
- Performance loss
- Stored VANOS codes

## What to check in logs

- VANOS target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Clean/replace solenoids and check oil before tuning.

# E40 (Stock Turbo)

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

An E40 stock-turbo path runs roughly 40% ethanol on the factory turbos. Higher ethanol than E30 means more knock margin but more fuel demand.

## Why it matters

As ethanol rises, fuel-system capacity (LPFP/HPFP/injectors) becomes the limit before the turbos in many cases.

## How it works

A build path is a coherent combination of fuel, hardware, and a matching calibration. This wiki describes the concept and prerequisites; it does not publish tune values.

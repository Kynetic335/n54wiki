# LPFP Failure

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

LPFP failure or weakness starves the HPFP, causing fuel pressure problems under load.

## Why it matters

A weak LPFP is a fuel-supply limit, especially on high-power or high-ethanol builds.

## How it works

Insufficient low-side supply means the HPFP cannot maintain rail pressure.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Fuel pressure drop under load
- Lean condition
- Limp mode

## What to check in logs

- LPFP pressure
- Fuel rail pressure target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Test low-side supply; replace/upgrade as the build needs.

## Related codes or fault patterns

- Fuel-pressure plausibility faults (e.g. 2AAF) — verify by scan.

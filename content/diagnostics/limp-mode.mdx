# Limp Mode

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Limp mode is a protective state where the DME drastically reduces power after detecting a fault (boost, fuel pressure, or other).

## Why it matters

It protects the engine; the goal is to find and fix the underlying fault, not to mask it.

## How it works

On a serious fault, the DME limits boost/torque and may close the throttle until restarted or fixed.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Sudden major power loss
- CEL
- Reduced rev/boost

## What to check in logs

- Fault codes
- Boost target vs actual
- Fuel rail pressure

## Mechanical checks first

Scan for the triggering code and fix the root cause.

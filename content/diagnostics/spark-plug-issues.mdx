# Spark Plug Issues

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Spark plug issues (wear, wrong gap, wrong heat range) cause misfires that worsen under boost.

## Why it matters

Plugs are a top cause of "tune" misfires; correct gap is critical as boost rises.

## How it works

A worn or over-gapped plug cannot maintain spark under high cylinder pressure.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Misfire under boost
- Power loss at the top

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters
- Timing corrections

## Mechanical checks first

Inspect, re-gap, or replace plugs to spec before tuning. Verify gap by source.

# Electric Water Pump

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The N54 uses an electric water pump (rather than a belt-driven one) to circulate coolant. It is a well-known failure item.

## Why it matters

Water pump failure causes overheating and can strand the car. It is a top reliability concern, especially before raising power.

## How it works

An electric motor drives the coolant pump; the DME controls it based on temperature needs.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Overheating
- Coolant temperature warnings
- Limp mode on overheat

## What to check in logs

- Coolant temp

## Mechanical checks first

Verify water pump function and age; replace proactively before tuning if due.

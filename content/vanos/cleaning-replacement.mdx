# VANOS Cleaning / Replacement

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Servicing VANOS usually means cleaning or replacing the solenoids and ensuring clean oil; mechanical units are replaced if worn.

## Why it matters

Many VANOS faults resolve with solenoid cleaning/replacement and proper oil — far cheaper than chasing them in the tune.

## How it works

Solenoids are removed, cleaned or replaced, and oil/maintenance is addressed to restore correct actuation.

- VANOS codes that clear after service

## What to check in logs

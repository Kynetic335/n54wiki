# 2ABC / 3100 Boost Faults

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

2ABC and 3100 are commonly referenced N54 boost-related fault codes. Confirm exact definitions by scanning your car.

## Why it matters

These point to boost-control or boost-deviation issues, usually mechanical (leaks, vacuum, wastegates/solenoids).

## How it works

When boost behavior deviates from expected, the DME logs the relevant code.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Underboost or boost-control problems
- Possible limp mode

## What to check in logs

- Boost target vs actual
- WGDC

## Mechanical checks first

Inspect charge path, vacuum lines, solenoids, and wastegates before tuning.

# Logging Oil Temp

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Oil temperature reflects how hard the engine is working and cooling adequacy. Sustained high oil temps reduce protection.

## Why it matters

Oil temp context matters for hard/track use and deciding on cooling upgrades.

## How it works

A sensor reports oil temp; it climbs with sustained load.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- High oil temps under sustained use

## What to check in logs

- Oil temp

## Mechanical checks first

Ensure correct oil and cooling capacity for the intended use.

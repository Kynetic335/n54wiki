# Daily Reliable

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A daily-reliable path prioritizes longevity and drivability over peak numbers: maintenance current, conservative targets, and good cooling/fueling margin.

## Why it matters

The most common real-world goal. A reliable daily is mostly about maintenance and conservative calibration, not chasing peak power.

## How it works

A build path is a coherent combination of fuel, hardware, and a matching calibration. This wiki describes the concept and prerequisites; it does not publish tune values.

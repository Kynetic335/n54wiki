# Logging Coolant Temp

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Coolant temperature is a core health channel. Rising coolant temps under load can trigger protection and timing pull.

## Why it matters

It reveals cooling-system health and explains power loss from heat.

## How it works

A sensor reports coolant temp; the DME manages cooling and protection around it.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Climbing coolant temp under load

## What to check in logs

- Coolant temp

## Mechanical checks first

Confirm water pump/thermostat/radiator health before blaming the tune.

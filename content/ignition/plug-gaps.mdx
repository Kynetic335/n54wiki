# Plug Gaps

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Plug gap is the distance the spark jumps. Higher cylinder pressure (more boost) makes the spark harder to maintain, so tuned N54s commonly run a smaller gap. Exact gap depends on the build — verify by source.

## Why it matters

Too large a gap under boost causes spark blowout and misfires; correct gap keeps combustion stable.

## How it works

Smaller gaps need less voltage to jump, helping spark survive high cylinder pressure.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Misfire that worsens with boost
- Power loss at the top

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters
- Timing corrections

## Mechanical checks first

Set and verify gap to spec for the build before tuning. Source needed for exact values.

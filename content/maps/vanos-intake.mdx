# VANOS Intake Map

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

> Educational reference only. This page does not publish tune values.

## What this map controls

The VANOS intake map commands target intake cam angles across rpm and load, affecting airflow and spool.

## Why it matters

Cam timing affects spool and torque shape. Mis-set timing hurts drivability or triggers VANOS faults.

## Related log channels

- Intake VANOS target vs actual
- Boost target vs actual

## What changes are visible in logs

Changes show as different commanded intake cam angles and altered spool/torque.

## What can go wrong if changed blindly

- Rough running
- VANOS deviation faults
- Worse spool

## Mechanical problems that can look like a tuning problem

- Dirty/failed solenoids
- Low/dirty oil
- Mechanical VANOS wear

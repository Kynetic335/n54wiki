# Oil Pan Gasket

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The oil pan gasket seals the oil pan to the block. It can leak with age and is a more involved repair due to access.

## Why it matters

A leaking pan gasket drips oil and is labor-intensive to fix because of subframe/access considerations.

## How it works

The gasket fails with age/heat, leaking oil at the pan.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Oil drips from the lower engine
- Oil pooling under the car

Confirm the source is the pan gasket (not a higher leak running down) before the repair.

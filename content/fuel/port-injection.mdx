# Port Injection (Add-On)

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Port injection (PI) adds injectors in the intake ports to supplement the factory direct injection on high-power builds. The N54 is DI-only from the factory; PI is an aftermarket addition.

## Why it matters

On builds that exceed direct-injection fuel capacity, port injection adds fuel headroom and can help keep intake valves cleaner.

## How it works

A separate PI system meters additional fuel into the ports, supplementing the DI system at high demand.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- DI fuel system at its limit on big builds (the reason PI is added)

## What to check in logs

- Rail pressure target vs actual
- Fuel trims
- PI system pressure if equipped

## Mechanical checks first

PI is a hardware addition; verify the install and control strategy. This wiki does not provide tuning values.

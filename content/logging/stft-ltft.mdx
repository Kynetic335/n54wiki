# Logging STFT / LTFT

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Fuel trims (short-term and long-term) show how much the DME is adjusting fueling to hit its target. Large trims indicate the base fueling is off.

## Why it matters

Trims reveal vacuum leaks, fueling drift, and sensor issues; they provide context for lean/rich complaints.

## How it works

The DME corrects fueling based on feedback; STFT reacts quickly, LTFT learns over time.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Large positive trims (lean correction, possible leak)
- Large negative trims (rich correction)

## What to check in logs

- STFT
- LTFT
- Lambda/AFR

## Mechanical checks first

Check for vacuum/intake leaks and sensor health when trims are large.

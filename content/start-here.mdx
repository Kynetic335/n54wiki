# Start Here

New to the N54 or just beginning your tuning journey? This page maps out the right order to read the wiki based on where you are right now.

---

## Step 1 — Understand the Engine

Before tuning anything, understand what you're working with. The N54 is a capable platform but has specific maintenance requirements that **must be addressed before adding power**.

- **[N54 Engine Overview](/engine)** — displacement, architecture, stock power figures, and common failure modes
- **[MSD80 vs MSD81](/engine/msd80-vs-msd81)** — which ECU your car has and what it means for tuning

## Step 2 — Get the Car Healthy

A tune on a neglected car is a recipe for expensive problems. Spark plugs, injectors, and cooling system health matter more than many people realize.

- **[Maintenance Before Tuning](/maintenance/before-tuning)** — the service checklist every N54 owner should complete before flashing

## Step 3 — Pick Your Upgrade Path

The N54 has well-documented stage tiers from bolt-on modifications to full turbo swaps.

- **[Stage 1](/tuning/stage-1)** — software-only or minor bolt-ons, no supporting mods required
- **[Stage 2](/tuning/stage-2)** — downpipe, intake, FMIC — meaningful power gains with a custom tune
- **[Stage 2+](/tuning/stage-2-plus)** — TMIC upgrade, methanol injection, pushing the stock turbos further
- **[Hybrid Turbos](/tuning/hybrid-twins)** — upgraded twin-scroll turbos for more airflow on the stock location
- **[Single Turbo](/tuning/single-turbo)** — the top tier: big power, longer spool, fully built required

## Step 4 — Address Fueling

Every stage beyond Stage 1 puts increased demand on the fuel system. Understand what you're running before you tune.

- **[Fuel Grades: 91 / 93 / E30 / E50 / E85](/fueling)** — fuel quality, octane, and ethanol blending
- **[HPFP / LPFP / Port Injection](/fueling/hpfp-lpfp-port-injection)** — how the N54 fuel system works and when it becomes the limiting factor

## Step 5 — Check Your Hardware

- **[Spark Plugs / Coils / Injectors](/hardware/spark-plugs-coils-injectors)** — what to upgrade, when, and to what spec

## Step 6 — Learn to Datalog

Datalogging is non-negotiable on a modified N54. It's how you catch problems before they become engine damage.

- **[Datalogging Guide](/datalogging/guide)** — what app to use, what channels to log, and how often
- **[MHD Log Channel Guide](/datalogging/mhd-channels)** — channel-by-channel reference for MHD users
- **[Boost Control / WGDC](/datalogging/boost-wgdc)** — how boost control works and how to read WGDC logs
- **[Timing Corrections / Knock](/datalogging/timing-knock)** — the most important numbers in your log
- **[AFR / Lambda / Fuel Trims](/datalogging/afr-lambda-fuel-trims)** — interpreting fuel system feedback

## Step 7 — Diagnose Problems

- **[Common Codes: 30FF, Misfires & More](/diagnostics/common-codes)** — fault code reference for the most common N54 issues

## Step 8 — Run the Pre-Tune Checklist

- **[Tune Readiness Checklist](/checklist/tune-readiness)** — a structured checklist to go through before scheduling or purchasing a tune

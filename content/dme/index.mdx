# DME / ROM

The DME (Digital Motor Electronics) is the N54 engine computer. This section covers the MSD80 and MSD81 control units, the ROM images they run, how BIN files and XDF definitions relate, and why checksums and ROM matching matter. This wiki explains these concepts; it does not host private BIN/XDF files or any flashing workflow.

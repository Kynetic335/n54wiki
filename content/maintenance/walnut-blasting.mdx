# Walnut Blasting

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Walnut blasting cleans carbon buildup off the intake valves. Because the N54 is direct-injection, no fuel washes the valves, so carbon accumulates over time.

## Why it matters

Carbon buildup hurts airflow, idle quality, and power; periodic cleaning restores it.

## How it works

Crushed walnut media is blasted against the valves to remove carbon deposits.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Rough idle
- Reduced power
- Misfire from heavy buildup

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters
- Fuel trims (context)

## Mechanical checks first

A mechanical cleaning task; periodic on DI engines.

# Cooling / Oil

The Cooling / Oil section covers the systems that keep the N54 alive under load: the electric water pump, thermostat, radiator, and oil cooler, plus coolant and oil temperature behavior, heat soak, and the common leak points (valve cover gasket, oil filter housing gasket, oil pan gasket). Cooling and oil health directly affect how hard the engine can be run safely.

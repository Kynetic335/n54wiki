# Intake Cam Timing

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Intake cam timing is the VANOS-controlled position of the intake camshaft, adjusted to optimize airflow across the rev range.

## Why it matters

Intake timing affects spool and power delivery; incorrect actual-vs-target points at VANOS faults.

## How it works

The DME targets an intake cam angle; VANOS moves the cam via oil pressure and solenoid control.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Deviation between target and actual cam angle
- Rough running

## What to check in logs

- Intake VANOS target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Verify VANOS mechanical health before changing cam tables.

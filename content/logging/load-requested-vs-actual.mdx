# Load Requested vs Actual

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Requested vs actual load shows what load the DME is targeting versus what it achieves — the air-side equivalent of boost target vs actual.

## Why it matters

Actual lagging requested points at a boost shortfall (often mechanical) or a limiter; a capped request points at the tune.

## How it works

The torque request becomes a requested load; the engine works to achieve it; actual is measured.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Actual below requested (shortfall)
- Requested pinned at a ceiling (limiter)

## What to check in logs

- Requested load
- Actual load
- Boost target vs actual

## Mechanical checks first

Shortfall vs ceiling are different problems; smoke-test before assuming a limiter.

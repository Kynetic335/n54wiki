## What this map controls

Timing correction logic governs how the DME removes (and restores) advance in response to knock detection.

## Why it matters

It is a protection system. Tampering with it to hide knock removes safety and risks damage.

## Related log channels

- Timing corrections by cylinder
- IAT

## What changes are visible in logs

Changes show as different correction magnitude/behavior in logs.

## What can go wrong if changed blindly

- Masking real knock
- Engine damage
- False sense of safety

## Mechanical problems that can look like a tuning problem

- Fuel quality
- Heat

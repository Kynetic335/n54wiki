# PCV System

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

The PCV (positive crankcase ventilation) system manages crankcase pressure and vapors. N54 PCV components can fail and cause leaks or vacuum issues.

## Why it matters

A failed PCV can cause oil consumption, rough running, smoke, and vacuum-related drivability problems.

## How it works

The PCV routes crankcase vapors back to the intake; failures upset crankcase pressure.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Oil consumption
- Smoke
- Rough running
- Whistling

## What to check in logs

- Fuel trims (context)

## Mechanical checks first

Inspect/replace PCV components; verify for leaks.

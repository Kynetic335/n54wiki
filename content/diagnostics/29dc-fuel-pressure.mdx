# 29DC Fuel Pressure

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

29DC is a commonly referenced N54 fuel-pressure fault. Confirm the exact definition by scanning your car.

## Why it matters

It typically points to fuel-supply problems (HPFP/LPFP) and is associated with long crank and pressure crashes.

## How it works

When rail pressure cannot meet target/plausibility, the DME logs the fault.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Long crank
- Rail pressure crash
- Limp mode

## What to check in logs

- Fuel rail pressure target vs actual
- LPFP pressure

## Mechanical checks first

Check HPFP/LPFP; usually a mechanical fuel-supply issue.

# Maps

The Maps section explains what the major N54 calibration maps control, the log channels used to validate them, and the problems that look like tuning issues but are really mechanical. These pages are educational: they explain concepts and do not publish real tune values, addresses, or copy-paste tables.

## Pages in this section

- **[Load Target Map](/wiki/maps/load-target)** — Targets engine load.
- **[Boost Target Map](/wiki/maps/boost-target)** — Targets manifold boost.
- **[WGDC Base Map](/wiki/maps/wgdc-base)** — Base wastegate duty cycle.
- **[WGDC Correction Map](/wiki/maps/wgdc-correction)** — Closed-loop boost trim.
- **[Boost Limiters](/wiki/maps/boost-limiters)** — Protective boost caps.
- **[Torque Limiters](/wiki/maps/torque-limiters)** — Caps on torque request/output.
- **[Driver Demand Map](/wiki/maps/driver-demand)** — Pedal-to-torque mapping.
- **[Main Ignition Timing](/wiki/maps/main-ignition-timing)** — Base spark advance.
- **[Timing Correction Map](/wiki/maps/timing-correction)** — Knock-related timing logic.
- **[Lambda Target Map](/wiki/maps/lambda-target)** — Commanded mixture.
- **[Fuel Scalar](/wiki/maps/fuel-scalar)** — Overall fueling scaling.
- **[Fuel Pressure Target](/wiki/maps/fuel-pressure-target)** — Commanded rail pressure.
- **[VANOS Intake Map](/wiki/maps/vanos-intake)** — Intake cam timing targets.
- **[VANOS Exhaust Map](/wiki/maps/vanos-exhaust)** — Exhaust cam timing targets.
- **[Throttle Torque Intervention](/wiki/maps/throttle-torque-intervention)** — Throttle-based torque control.
- **[MAP / TMAP Scaling](/wiki/maps/map-tmap-scaling)** — Sensor scaling for boost reading.
- **[Rev Limiters](/wiki/maps/rev-limiters)** — RPM limit logic.

# Fuel Pressure Target

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

> Educational reference only. This page does not publish tune values.

## What this map controls

The fuel pressure target map sets the rail pressure the HPFP is commanded to maintain across conditions.

## Why it matters

Targeting more pressure than the pump can supply causes a crash under load. The target must respect HPFP/LPFP capacity.

## Related log channels

- Fuel rail pressure target vs actual
- LPFP pressure

## What changes are visible in logs

Changes show as a different commanded rail pressure and whether actual can hold it.

## What can go wrong if changed blindly

- Pressure crash if beyond pump capacity
- Lean under load
- Limp mode

## Mechanical problems that can look like a tuning problem

- Worn HPFP/LPFP
- Supply restriction

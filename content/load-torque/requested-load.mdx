# Requested Load

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Requested load is the load target the DME calculates from the torque request and driver demand before limiters.

## Why it matters

Comparing requested vs actual load is the core check for whether the car is delivering what it intends to.

## How it works

Driver demand becomes a torque request, which becomes a requested load that boost/throttle work to achieve.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Requested load capped below expectation (limiter active)

## What to check in logs

- Requested load
- Actual load
- Boost target

## Mechanical checks first

If requested load itself is capped, look at limiters, not hardware.

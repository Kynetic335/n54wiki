# Stage 1 (91 Octane)

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

A Stage 1 path on 91-octane fuel: a calibration matched to lower-octane pump gas, typically with minimal or no supporting hardware. Octane availability varies by region.

## Why it matters

Fuel octane sets the safe limit. A 91 path is more conservative than 93 because lower octane has less knock margin.

## How it works

A build path is a coherent combination of fuel, hardware, and a matching calibration. This wiki describes the concept and prerequisites; it does not publish tune values.

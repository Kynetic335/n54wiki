## What it is

Load and torque are linked: the DME translates between airflow-based load and the torque the engine is expected to produce, using the torque model.

## Why it matters

Understanding the load-to-torque relationship explains why the DME manages air the way it does and how limiters affect output.

## How it works

The torque model relates achieved load (air) to expected torque, accounting for efficiency factors.

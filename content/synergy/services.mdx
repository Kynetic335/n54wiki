# Synergy BMW Tuning Services

This wiki is powered by **Synergy BMW Tuning** — a tuning operation focused exclusively on the BMW N54 platform and built on the belief that every N54 deserves a calibration based on the actual data from that specific car.

---

## Who We Are

Synergy BMW Tuning was founded by N54 enthusiasts who grew tired of off-the-shelf flash maps being sold as "custom tunes." Every build is different — fuel quality, turbo condition, hardware list, ambient climate, and driving style all affect what a good calibration looks like.

We work with N54 owners at every stage, from first-time flashers on a bone-stock 335i to full single-turbo builds on built bottom ends.

---

## Services

### Custom Remote Tune
A full calibration of your ECU, developed by reviewing your datalogs and iterating the tune to your specific car and hardware.

**What's included:**
- Baseline tune flash
- Log review and analysis
- Iterative revisions based on your feedback and log data
- Final calibration targeting your specified fuel and hardware list
- Support throughout the process

**Best for:** Anyone who wants more than a generic map. Stage 1 through single-turbo.

### Log Review
Send us your MHD logs. We'll give you a written analysis of what we see — timing corrections, fuel system health, boost behavior, and anything that warrants attention before you push harder.

**Best for:** Pre-purchase checks, verifying an existing tune is safe, diagnosing anomalies you're not sure how to read.

### Tune Readiness Consultation
Not sure if your car is ready to tune? Walk through the readiness checklist with us. We'll review your modification list, mileage, and any diagnostic history to identify what needs to happen before you flash.

**Best for:** First-time N54 tuners; anyone who wants a professional second opinion before spending money on hardware or a tune.

### Calibration Revision
Already have a tune from a previous tuner or off-the-shelf map? We can review your logs and revise the calibration to work correctly for your current hardware and fuel setup.

---

## What to Expect

We take a data-first approach. Before we touch a calibration table, we want to see logs from your car at its current state. This means:

1. You provide logs (MHD is preferred)
2. We review and identify any hardware issues that need to be addressed first
3. We flash a starting calibration
4. You log pulls and send them back
5. We revise until the car is clean — no knock, correct fuel delivery, stable boost

This process typically takes several log sessions over days to weeks. Good tuning isn't instant.

# Logging Boost Control Problems

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Boost-control problems show up as actual boost missing target, oscillation, or WGDC behaving abnormally relative to boost error.

## Why it matters

Reading WGDC against boost target vs actual separates mechanical issues (leaks, vacuum, wastegates) from tuning.

## How it works

Healthy control tracks target with sensible WGDC; problems show as pegged WGDC, oscillation, or persistent error.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Underboost
- Overboost
- Oscillation
- WGDC pegged

## What to check in logs

- Boost target vs actual
- WGDC

## Mechanical checks first

High WGDC + low boost = mechanical/leak. Smoke-test before tuning.

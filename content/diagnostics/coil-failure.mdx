# Coil Failure

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Coil failure causes misfires, especially under boost, and is a routine N54 wear item.

## Why it matters

A weak coil cannot reliably fire under high cylinder pressure, causing load-dependent misfires.

## How it works

The coil degrades and fires weakly; the affected cylinder misfires under load.

## Common symptoms or failure modes

- Misfire under boost
- Rough idle
- Stumble

## What to check in logs

- Misfire counters by cylinder

## Mechanical checks first

Swap-test coils to isolate; replace failing units.

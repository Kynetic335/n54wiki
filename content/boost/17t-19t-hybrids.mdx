# 17T / 19T Hybrids

> **Source confidence:** Needs validation

## What it is

Hybrid turbos (commonly referred to as 17T, 19T, etc.) upgrade the stock-location twin turbos with larger compressor wheels for more airflow while keeping a twin-turbo layout.

## Why it matters

Hybrids raise the airflow ceiling beyond stock while retaining relatively quick spool — a popular middle step before single turbo.

## How it works

Larger wheels flow more air; boost control and fueling must be matched to the new capacity.
